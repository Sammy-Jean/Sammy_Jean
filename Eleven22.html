The Church of Eleven22

The website underwent a complete redesign and build, leveraging Bootstrap, The Rock CMS, traditional CSS, JavaScript, API calls, and custom development. A small team successfully developed over 150 pages, and as the lead designer and researcher for the project, I took charge of overseeing the visual aspects and user experience of the website. Collaborating closely with the team, I provided guidance and direction in creating a cohesive and visually appealing design language that aligned with the project's goals.
