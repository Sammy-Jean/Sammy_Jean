Joaquin Niemann's Story Promo

Joaquin Niemann, a prominent PGA Tour player, became the focus of this project aimed at promoting an upcoming player highlight series. The primary objective was to create a captivating promo that would captivate the audience. Surprisingly, the promo garnered a significant amount of interest when shared on various social media platforms. In this endeavor, my role involved assisting in the editing process, curating compelling content, and offering valuable insights on how to effectively present the piece on social media. By collaborating on these aspects, we successfully crafted an engaging promo that effectively captured the attention of viewers and generated considerable buzz.
